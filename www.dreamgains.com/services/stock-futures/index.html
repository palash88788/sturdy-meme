About

Stock Futures is one of the most rewarding Instruments traded in the NSE. In India, NSE is a stock exchange where most of the trading volume is generated in Derivatives segment and specifically the futures market. Intraday turnover is the biggest in NSE when compared to any other stock exchange in India.

Excited about trading in the Futures Market, if you feel that you are a seasoned trader and want to now diversify and put your eggs into different segments then trading in Stock Futures is ideal. Derivatives and specifically futures are relatively new financial instruments in the Indian stock market. However, this product generates the highest number of trades, it being the instrument the financial institutions trade in India.

You will receive Intraday tips (one day/less than one day holding period) on stock futures through SMS and online chat-room services wherein strive to maintain an accuracy of 80-85% on a consistent basis.
